About Us

Our Mission

At Jupietr, we are dedicated to providing quality education that empowers students to reach their full potential. Our mission is to foster a love for learning and to equip our students with the skills they need to succeed in a rapidly changing world.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of honesty and transparency.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in everything we do.
  • Inclusivity: We embrace diversity and promote an inclusive environment.
  • Innovation: We encourage creativity and innovative thinking.

Why Choose Us?

Our experienced educators are passionate about teaching and are committed to nurturing young minds. We offer a variety of programs tailored to meet the needs of our students, ensuring that each child receives personalized attention and support.

Join us in shaping the future leaders of tomorrow!

What Courses We Offer ?

At Affan's Education Center, we provide comprehensive education from classes 3 to 12, catering to various educational boards.

Classes 3 to 5

Foundational education focusing on core subjects to build a strong base.

Classes 6 to 8

Intermediate education with an emphasis on critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Classes 9 to 10
